About me

I’m a curious self-taught developer with a strong passion for IoT and web technologies. Born in Magelang and raised in Karawang, I recently graduated from vocational high school and now spend most of my time exploring personal tech projects — from web apps to microcontroller automation.
Outside of coding, I enjoy tinkering with old electronics, creating useful things out of leftovers, and immersing myself in the peaceful world of Minecraft with shaders on. I believe learning is not about rushing to the top, but growing steadily with each line of code.
I dream of building meaningful tools— no matter how small — that make life just a little better for others.
